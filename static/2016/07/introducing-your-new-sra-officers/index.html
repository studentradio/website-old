Introducing your new SRA Officers…

 22nd July 2016 |  Members News, SRA Updates, Uncategorised

Back in April, in Cardiff, at #SRACon, there was a conference. And in that conference, there was an AGM. And within that AGM, you elected your new officers to look after the SRA this year! As is only right and good, it is appropriate that we introduce ourselves to you.

And what better way to do that than a series of tweets. Am I right?

No?

Meh, well, I’m doing it anyway. Let’s start with the Exec:

 

Exec Officers – managing the day-to-day running of the SRA.

 

Chair – Jonty Usborne (University Radio Bath)

The spokesperson of the SRA, the Chair oversees and coordinates the work of the SRA and is the main representative for the organisation to the industry and other external stakeholders.

My acceptance letter to, and graduation certificate from, @UniofBath. Celebrating 40 & 50 years. It’s been a while. pic.twitter.com/HCs3fvv78W

— Jonty Usborne (@JontyUsborne) July 4, 2016

 

Membership and Communications – Dom Stirling (Spark FM)

Responsible for all the administration of the SRA station membership, the newsfeed and social media – the Membership and Communications Officer is the first port of call for all members.

Got a busy studio this morning with @Toolyboy @BrookeLVincent AND @ollyofficial presenting @KEY103 Breakfast! pic.twitter.com/ejN44yilKa

— Dom Stirling (@domstirling12) July 13, 2016

 

Training and Development – Matt Maddren (Fly FM)

The Training and Development Officer is here to provide help and support to our members as well as to coordinate the work of our Regional Officers in the organising of our training days.

Events – Will Short (Xpression FM)

The Events Officer oversees the SRA’s flagship events, most notably the Student Radio Awards, supported by BBC Radio 1 and Global.

Responsible for sponsorship, the Marketing Officer works to ensure the organisation continues to be financially sufficient and to bring new opportunities to members through advertising.
