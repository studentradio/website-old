The UK Student Radio Association is an organisation run to enable, encourage and act on behalf of the UK student radio community. We represent radio stations linked to educational establishments, as well as individual students participating in radio and audio projects, and work with industry bodies to offer advice, support and opportunities to our members. Learn how you can be a part of what we do.
